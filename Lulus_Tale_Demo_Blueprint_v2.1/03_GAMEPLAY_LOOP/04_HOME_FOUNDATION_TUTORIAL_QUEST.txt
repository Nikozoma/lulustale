HOME FOUNDATION TUTORIAL QUEST

Purpose:
Create the smallest real-asset-backed playable slice that proves Lulu's Tale can be built correctly before expanding to the rest of Day 1.

Quest ID suggestion:
home_foundation_day1

Required flags:
- hasDogFood = false/true
- fridgeInteracted = false/true
- dogFed = false/true
- homeFoundationComplete = false/true

Suggested objective sequence:
1. "Move around Lulu's room."
2. "Go to the fridge."
3. "Get the dog food."
4. "Feed the dog."
5. "Go to the front door."
6. "Foundation complete." or "Ready to leave home." depending on implementation stage.

Required interactions:
Fridge:
- Trigger: Lulu close enough to fridge_interaction marker.
- Action: tap/interact.
- Result: hasDogFood = true, fridgeInteracted = true.

Dog/dog bowl:
- Trigger: Lulu close enough to dog_interaction or dog_bowl marker.
- Requires: hasDogFood = true.
- Result: dogFed = true.

Exit door:
- Trigger: Lulu close enough to entrance_exit marker.
- During foundation-only build: show a message instead of transitioning outside unless explicitly requested.

Dialogue tone:
Short, readable, lightly comedic. Keep it simple for the foundation.

Example text:
- Lulu: "Alright. New day. Same apartment. Suspiciously same dog."
- System: "Use the left side of the screen to move."
- System: "Tap nearby things to interact."
- Lulu: "Dog food acquired. The fridge remains undefeated."
- Dog: "..."
- Lulu: "You are welcome, tiny landlord."
