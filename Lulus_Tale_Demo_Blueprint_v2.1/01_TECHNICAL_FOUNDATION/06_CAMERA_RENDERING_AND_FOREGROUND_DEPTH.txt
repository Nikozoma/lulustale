CAMERA, RENDERING, AND FOREGROUND DEPTH

Camera:
- Top-down scrolling camera.
- Camera centers on Lulu.
- Camera is constrained at map edges.
- Smooth enough for mobile readability.

Draw order:
1. background/base layer
2. mid-depth objects
3. player/entities
4. foreground objects where needed
5. UI

Foreground visibility:
- Large objects that can hide Lulu must not fully obscure the player.
- Use either split foreground/background layers or fade-on-overlap.
- Collision bounds and visibility/fade bounds must be separate.

Demo proof:
- At least one bush, tree, sign, or building-edge object should demonstrate visibility handling if feasible.
- Good candidates:
  - bush near fries recovery spot,
  - tree near overworld route,
  - exterior sign/building overhang.

Do not overbuild:
- A simple opacity fade is enough for the demo.
- Full y-sorted depth rendering can be improved later.
