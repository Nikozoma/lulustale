INTERACTION PROXIMITY RULES

Events and NPC/object interactions require Lulu to be close enough.

Core rule:
The player may tap an NPC/object/event target, but it only activates if Lulu is within that target's interaction radius.

Required interactables:
- Fridge
- Dog Bowl or Dog
- Charles Jr. cashier counter/order point
- Fries object during recovery
- Bed
- Window during night interruption
- Sword bush during Pass 2
- Entrance/exit transition zones

Interaction object structure:
- id
- position or bounds
- interactionRadius or interactionBounds
- enabled condition
- prompt text
- onInteract handler

Player feedback:
- When Lulu is near an interactable, show a small prompt such as "Tap to interact".
- If the player taps from too far away, do nothing.
- Do not allow long-distance interaction.
