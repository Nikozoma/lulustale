OVERWORLD LAYOUT RULES

Reference:
01_overworld_map_layout.png

Purpose:
Supports walking from home to Charles Jr., bird theft, bird chase, fries recovery, and later night confrontation.

Color meaning:
- Gray = roads, parking lot, drive lane.
- Yellow = sidewalks/curbs.
- Green = grass/open lawn.
- Pink = Charles Jr.
- Blue = Player Apartment/buildings.
- Purple = Player Door.
- Orange = Apartment 2.
- Black/dark border = out-of-bounds.

Specific map rules:
- Left side of Charles Jr. is a parking lot.
- Right side of Charles Jr. is a thin drive-through lane/normal road, not a parking lot.
- Bottom areas not used for roads/buildings should remain grass.
- Use the top connector route between Charles Jr. and apartment complex for return path.
- Bird theft should trigger before Lulu reaches the apartment driveway/entrance.
- Bird chase should lead to apartment complex right-side street, all the way toward the bottom.

Required zones:
- PlayerDoorTransitionZone
- CharlesJrEntranceTransitionZone
- CharlesJrReturnRouteTriggerZone
- BirdTheftTriggerZone
- BirdChaseWaypointZones
- FriesRecoverySpot
- BushHideSpot
- OptionalSwordBushSpot for Pass 2
- ApartmentReturnZone
- NightBirdConfrontationZone for Pass 2
