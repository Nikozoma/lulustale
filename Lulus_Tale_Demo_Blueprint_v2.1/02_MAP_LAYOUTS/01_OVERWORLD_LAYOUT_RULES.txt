OVERWORLD LAYOUT RULES

Sources of truth:
- Overworld.json
- Overworld.visual.json
- public/assets/maps/overworld/overworld_walkbehind_base_96x68.png
- public/assets/maps/overworld/foreground/overworld_building_upper_foreground_96x68.png
- public/assets/maps/overworld/foreground/overworld_tree_canopy_foreground_96x68.png
- public/assets/maps/overworld/overworld_96x68_manifest.json

Map scale:
- 96 tiles wide.
- 68 tiles tall.
- 32 game-world pixels per tile.
- 3072 x 2176 world pixels total.
- This is an exact 2x-width/2x-height nearest-neighbor enlargement of the
  approved 48 x 34 composition; no camera, player, movement, or tile-size
  scaling is used.

Purpose:
Supports walking from home to Charles Jr., bird theft, bird chase, fries recovery, and later night confrontation.

Specific map rules:
- Left side of Charles Jr. is a parking lot.
- Right side of Charles Jr. is a thin drive-through lane/normal road, not a parking lot.
- Bottom areas not used for roads/buildings should remain grass.
- Use the top connector route between Charles Jr. and apartment complex for return path.
- Quest events must use reachable semantic markers on open paths/parking/grass.
- Do not place objectives inside the closed lower-right dirt-lot fence.

Required zones:
- PlayerDoorTransitionZone
- CharlesJrEntranceTransitionZone
- CharlesJrReturnRouteTriggerZone
- BirdTheftTriggerZone
- BirdChaseWaypointZones
- FriesRecoverySpot
- BushHideSpot
- OptionalSwordBushSpot for Pass 2
- ApartmentReturnZone
- NightBirdConfrontationZone for Pass 2

Foreground depth:
- Tree canopies anchored by semantic tree objects are provided by the active transparent foreground overlay.
- Keep tree collision in Overworld.json separate from the canopy visual layer.
- The four enlarged rows corresponding to the original upper two rows of
  Charles Jr. and all three apartment buildings are walk-behind zones.
- Apartment zones use grass beneath them; Charles Jr. uses parking-lot asphalt.
- Lower building rows remain solid.
- Building foreground sections fade to 42% only while Lulu's feet are inside
  that building's four-row enlarged zone.
- The opaque normal composite must remain pixel-identical to the approved
  Overworld source.
